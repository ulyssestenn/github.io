Guten — Privacy Policy

Guten Privacy Policy

Guten is a reading app for public-domain books, designed to minimize data collection and keep your reading data under your control.

What Guten uses the internet for

Guten uses network access to fetch book listings, metadata, and downloadable book files from third-party public-domain sources, including Gutendex and Project Gutenberg.

What Guten does not do

  • No user accounts
  • No in-app advertising
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs

Data stored on your device

Your downloaded books and reading data (such as bookmarks, highlights, notes, and reading progress) are stored locally on your device.

When data may leave your device

Data leaves your device only when you take an explicit action, such as:

  • opening external links,
  • sharing content through the system share menu, or
  • exporting/backing up your data.

Third-party services

When Guten connects to external services (for example, Gutendex or Project Gutenberg), those services may receive standard technical request data (such as IP address and request headers) as part of normal internet communication. Their privacy policies apply to that processing.

External websites

If you open links to third-party websites, those sites operate under their own privacy policies, independent of Guten.
